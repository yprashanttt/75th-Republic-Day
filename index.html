75th Indian Republic Day

Celebrating 75 Years of Unity, Diversity, and Progress.

Two all-women contingents from the defence forces are set to participate in this year's Republic Day parade.

President Emmanuel Macron of France will grace the occasion as the esteemed Chief Guest, fostering international camaraderie.

In this year's Republic Day parade, a 95-member contingent and a 33-member band contingent from France will march down the majestic Kartavya Path on January 26. The French Foreign Legion contingent will also include six Indians.
